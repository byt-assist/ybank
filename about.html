🏦 About YBANK

YBANK is a digital asset on the TON blockchain. Fixed supply. No mint. No freeze. Ownership revoked.

YBANK is part of the BYT Assist ecosystem — a regulated digital asset platform backed by real-world collateral and institutional-grade infrastructure.

Mission

To provide a simple, secure, and transparent digital asset on TON.

Team

YBANK is operated by CEBIL UAE, a company registered in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC).
